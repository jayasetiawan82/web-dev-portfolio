03 about me

My professional journey began in hospitality as a guest service agent and as a chef. However, the lifestyle didn't resonate with me. Technology has always captivated me since a young age, pushing me to embrace a continuous learning mindset. Wanting to get new skills, I started a self-taught coding journey through Codecademy, Scrimba, and Udemy.

Taking a next step, I enrolled in a Certificate IV in IT, specializing in web development at Lumify Learn. While navigating the challenges of independent learning, I found satisfaction in the process.

I've had a wonderful career in hospitality and culinary arts, where I've learned to work in teams to keep calm under pressure, follow good work ethic, and how to be a kind human. However, In this short life I'd like to keep learning and evolving. Hence, I'm on the lookout for junior roles where I can put my skills to the test.
